About Elone

Our story, mission, and team

Crafting Spaces Since 2018

Elone began with a simple belief: everyone deserves beautiful, well-made furniture. From our workshop in Hyderabad, we design and deliver pieces that blend Pakistani craftsmanship with contemporary aesthetics.

Today we serve hundreds of homes with custom sofas, dining sets, bedroom furniture, and office solutions — all backed by a 5-year quality guarantee.
